  • Pop the oppositions' SPECIAL three balloons, maybe I'll write special on 'em, make it real obvious

  • Carry the oppositions' golden egg on the provided silver spoon with either Neil's or Tom's name on it across half. Eight ball pool rules: you break it your team loses
